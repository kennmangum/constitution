## 7-Day Pacing Rhythm

### Monday: Reconnect
- **Action**: Review current client status (where are they?)
- **Connection**: Reach out to 1 existing client (check-in, not sales)
- **Anchor**: "What matters this week for [client name]?"

### Tuesday: Prospect
- **Action**: Identify 1 warm lead or referral partner
- **Connection**: Email, call, or LinkedIn outreach to 1 prospect
- **Anchor**: "Who might benefit from Acumatica + CFO clarity?"

### Wednesday: Deliver
- **Action**: Do actual client work (implementation, CFO analysis, etc.)
- **Connection**: Send something valuable to 1 client (insight, report, next step)
- **Anchor**: "What can I complete today that moves a client forward?"

### Thursday: Learn
- **Action**: Deepen one skill area (Acumatica feature, CFO technique, industry insight)
- **Connection**: Engage with 1 peer/expert (Acumatica community, CFO network)
- **Anchor**: "What did I learn this week that makes me better for clients?"

### Friday: Reflect
- **Action**: Weekly review (what moved forward? what stalled? what's next?)
- **Connection**: Gratitude message to 1 person who helped this week
- **Anchor**: "What went well? What do I carry into next week?"

### Saturday: Rest
- **Action**: No business development, no planning, no "should"
- **Connection**: Optional - respond if clients reach out, but no initiating
- **Anchor**: "The work will be there Monday. Today is for recovery."

### Sunday: Spaciousness
- **Action**: Light planning for Monday if it feels natural (not required)
- **Connection**: If inspiration strikes, jot it down. Don't act on it yet.
- **Anchor**: "What wants to emerge? What's the next right step?"
